stan guts

Music by Graham Sahagian

I've been writing & producing music for about 7 years and have been playing music my whole life. I also occasionally DJ. I started out as a Jazz Trombonist, and gradually began to teach myself piano & guitar.

The music I like to make is typically of the lo-fi electronic variety. My music is available on Soundcloud and Bandcamp under the moniker 'stan guts'; a reference to the Jazz legend Stan Getz and legendary manga & anime: Berserk. My DAW of choice is Ableton Live.
